🏛️ Patent Pending — USPTO

The AI-powered food scanning technology behind Mizan is protected by a provisional patent application filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) under DTH Technology SARL. Patent covers multi-modal food recognition, calorie estimation, and predictive nutrition management.

Technology

Mizan is built on React, powered by Supabase for real-time sync, and uses Claude (Anthropic) for AI food recognition. Nutrition data comes from the USDA FoodData Central database — one of the most comprehensive nutrition databases in the world.
