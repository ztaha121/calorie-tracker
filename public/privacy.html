Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Mizan ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by ZAYT Digital / DTH Technology SARL. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Mizan app.

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use Mizan:

  • Account information: Your email address and name when you create an account.
  • Food logs: The meals, calories, and nutrition data you log in the app.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics about how you use the app (screen visits, feature usage).
  • Images: Photos you take for AI food scanning. These are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not stored by us.
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your card details.

How we use your information

  • To provide and sync your food logs across devices
  • To power the AI food scanning feature
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To send you account-related emails (receipts, password resets)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure. Food logs and account data are stored in encrypted databases. We use Row Level Security to ensure you can only access your own data.

AI food scanning

When you use the AI scan feature, your food photo is sent to Anthropic's API (Claude) for analysis. The photo is used only to identify the food and estimate nutrition — it is not stored by Anthropic beyond the API call. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic — AI food scanning (privacy policy)
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • USDA FoodData Central — nutrition database (public data, no personal info shared)
  • Vercel — app hosting (privacy policy)

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing zay.taha@gmail.com.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your food log data

Children's privacy

Mizan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at zay.taha@gmail.com.
